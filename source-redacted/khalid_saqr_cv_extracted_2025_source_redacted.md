## Executive Summary
Mechanical engineering professor and technology entrepreneur, expert in computational fluid
dynamics (CFD) and high-fidelity modelling and simulation. Led neurovascular hemodynamics research for Tohoku University (JAPAN), founded Bio-CFD Inc (USA) and KNOWDYN (UK). Key contributions include the discovery of non-Kolmogorov turbulence in human
circulation and the design of xPeerd. With a successful record of building international collaborations, securing support for research initiatives, and developing programs that connect
academic work with industry and innovation activities.
